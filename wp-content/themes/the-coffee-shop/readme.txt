Adsense is integrated for advertisement and affiliate marketing, this theme ensures your optional banners and ads shine. Whether you're a chef, cafe owner, review writer, food blogger, or running a fast food joint, this theme is your best ally. Boost your SEO with schema markup and make your site search engine friendly. The theme supports Woocommerce for eCommerce, perfect for selling coffee, cookies, wine, muffins, cakes, and vegan treats online. Ideal for startups, bars, bistros, cafeterias, tea house, diners, and boutiques, it’s a clean, simple, and effective choice for any dining experience.
